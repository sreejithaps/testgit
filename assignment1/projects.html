Topjobsoman

Topjobsoman is a job portal. It reaches out to all geographic areas of Oman and worldwide to provide a unique focal point for employers and jobseekers to meet. The site is designed for simplicity and ease of use. Site has 4 parts jobseekers, employer, situation wanted and admin. Jobseeker needs to register the site for applying the job and they can search job without registration. Employer should register the site for posting jobs and can purchase different products like featured job posting, magic job posting etc. Also, users can add classifieds in situation wanted after registering the site. PayPal is used for the payment.
